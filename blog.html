Updates

Backpacking Trip (June 22, 2019)

I just got back from a 5 day trip into the High Sierra. The snowpack is still super high, which made it very difficult to move at any real pace after noon - when the snow starts to get soft. Two high school buddies and I saw some incredible views, made it through a few dangerous situations, and had a great time reflecting on what matters to us. Hopefully I'll have a few more Sierra experiences this summer!

Scholar Athlete Banquet (May 20, 2019)

I won an award for having the highest GPA on my Cross Country team at UCLA.

Eye-Tracking Software (Fall 2018)

As part of my research with the Human-Computer Interaction lab at UCLA, I made a program that extracts eye images from live webcam footage. These images were then fed into a Deep Neural Network that estimated their gaze direction.
